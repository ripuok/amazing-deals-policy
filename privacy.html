Privacy Policy — Amazing Deals

Last updated: 10 December 2025

Amazing Deals ("we", "our", or "the app") provides tools to help users manage Facebook Pages, publish content, and interact with Page data using Meta’s APIs.

1. Information We Access

  • Page name, posts, insights
  • Content submitted (text, images, videos)
  • API logs, error logs

2. How We Use the Information

  • Publish posts on your Page
  • Retrieve insights and analytics
  • Maintain functionality of the app

3. Data Storage

We do not permanently store Facebook data except temporary tokens or logs if required.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share data with third parties. No selling, no advertising, no external use.

5. Permissions We Request

  • pages_manage_posts
  • pages_read_engagement
  • pages_show_list

6. User Control

You may revoke permissions anytime via Facebook → Settings → Business Integrations.

7. Security

We follow industry standard practices to protect tokens and data.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy and will reflect the new date above.
