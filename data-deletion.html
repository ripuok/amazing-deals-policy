Data Deletion Instructions — Amazing Deals

Amazing Deals does not store personal Facebook data permanently. However, if you want to remove all data associated with the app, follow the steps below.

Method 1 — Through Facebook Settings

You can delete your interaction data directly from Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps and Websites
  3. Find Amazing Deals
  4. Click Remove

This immediately revokes access and deletes related activity logs.

Method 2 — Request Deletion Manually

You may request deletion of any stored tokens or logs by emailing:

Email: ripu.ok@gmail.com

Your request will be processed within 48–72 hours.
